About Our Club




Our organization encourages fellowship and teamwork among wrestlers across Michigan Tech.


We aim to continue the folkstyle style of wrestling established across most high schools and help members of the wrestling club by providing weekly practices,


To promote staying physically fit,


To learn and master the vast variety of wrestling moves and techniques,


To provide an outlet for testing the skills and abilities of the wrestlers through live wrestling,


To promote and encourage the sport of wrestling across campus and aid all those with interest to become better individuals, students, and athletes,


And to encourage an all-around healthy wrestling lifestyle.
